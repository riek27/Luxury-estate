Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our painting, pressure washing, and epoxy services

How long does a typical painting project take?

Most interior painting projects take 2-5 days depending on room size and preparation needed. Exterior projects typically take 3-7 days. We provide detailed timelines with every estimate.

Do you use premium paint brands?

Yes, we primarily use Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore premium paints. These high-quality products ensure better coverage, durability, and long-lasting results for your investment.

Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces?

We use appropriate pressure levels for each surface. For delicate surfaces like roofs and older wood, we use soft washing techniques that clean effectively without causing damage.

How durable is epoxy flooring?

Our epoxy flooring systems are extremely durable, resisting chemicals, stains, and heavy impacts. With proper installation, they can last 10+ years even in high-traffic areas like garages.

Do I need to be home during the project?

For exterior work, you don't need to be home. For interior projects, we recommend someone being available for the initial setup and final walkthrough, but you don't need to stay throughout.

What's included in your free estimate?

Our free estimates include a detailed assessment of your project, recommended solutions, timeline, and a transparent breakdown of costs with no hidden fees or obligations.
