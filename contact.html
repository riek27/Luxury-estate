Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Legacy Builders.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope, complexity, and size. A custom luxury home typically takes 12-18 months from design to completion, while commercial projects can range from 18-36 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you work with client architects or require in-house design?

We're flexible! We have an exceptional in-house architectural team, but we also frequently collaborate with client-selected architects. Our goal is to ensure the best outcome for your project, regardless of the design source.

What is your approach to budgeting and cost management?

We believe in transparency and detailed budgeting from the outset. We provide comprehensive cost estimates and maintain open communication about any changes. Our track record includes 100% of projects completed within budget.

Do you handle permits and regulatory approvals?

Yes, we manage the entire permitting process, including applications, submissions, and coordination with local authorities. Our team has extensive experience navigating complex regulatory environments.

What regions do you serve?

While headquartered in New York, we undertake projects nationwide and internationally. We've completed luxury residences and commercial developments across the United States, Europe, and the Middle East.

How do you ensure quality throughout the construction process?

Quality is embedded in our process through rigorous material selection, skilled craftsmanship, continuous inspections, and detailed project management. We also provide regular updates and site walkthroughs so you can see the quality firsthand.
