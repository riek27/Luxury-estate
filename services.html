Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical painting project take?

Most interior painting projects take 2-5 days depending on room size and preparation needed. Exterior projects typically take 3-7 days. We provide detailed timelines with every estimate and work efficiently to complete your project on schedule.

Do you use premium paint brands?

Yes, we primarily use Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore premium paints. These high-quality products ensure better coverage, durability, and long-lasting results for your investment. We can also work with other premium brands based on your preference.

Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces?

We use appropriate pressure levels for each surface. For delicate surfaces like roofs and older wood, we use soft washing techniques that clean effectively without causing damage. Our technicians are trained to assess each surface and use the proper cleaning method.

How durable is epoxy flooring?

Our epoxy flooring systems are extremely durable, resisting chemicals, stains, and heavy impacts. With proper installation, they can last 10+ years even in high-traffic areas like garages. We include anti-slip additives for safety and UV-resistant top coats for longevity.

What's included in your free estimate?

Our free estimates include a detailed assessment of your project, recommended solutions, timeline, and a transparent breakdown of costs with no hidden fees or obligations. We take the time to understand your specific needs and provide a comprehensive proposal.
